Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Policy

M-SHWARI is committed to maintaining a secure financial platform and taking reasonable measures to help prevent money laundering, fraud, terrorist financing, and other unlawful financial activities.

Customer Identification

Users may be required to provide accurate personal information when registering for an account. We may request additional information where necessary to verify a user's identity and maintain account security.

Transaction Monitoring

To help protect the platform and our users, transactions may be monitored for unusual or suspicious activity. Reviews are conducted to support compliance with applicable laws and to safeguard the integrity of our services.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are prohibited on M-SHWARI:

  • Using another person's identity or account without authorization.
  • Attempting to conceal the origin of funds through unlawful means.
  • Using the platform for fraudulent, illegal, or unauthorized activities.
  • Providing false or misleading information during registration or verification.

Reporting Suspicious Activity

Where required by applicable law, M-SHWARI may report suspicious transactions or activities to the appropriate regulatory or law enforcement authorities.

Account Restrictions

Accounts found to be involved in prohibited activities may be temporarily restricted, suspended, or permanently closed in accordance with our Terms and Conditions and applicable legal requirements.

Policy Updates

This AML Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in applicable laws, regulations, or operational requirements. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of any updates.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this AML Policy, please contact our support team.
